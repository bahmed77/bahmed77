Air pollution in delhi is very hazardous.


The overall air quality index (AQI) in Delhi on Monday morning was recorded at 318 and remained in the ‘very poor’ category, according to air quality forecast agency SAFAR. The Supreme Court has started hearing on the rising air pollution matter in Delhi and nearby regions. The Delhi government has submitted an affidavit before the apex court and said, “Its ready to consider a complete lockdown to control the local emissions


The Supreme Court on Monday directed the National Task Force on air quality to hold a meeting tomorrow and suggest what can be done about the major factors contributing to air pollution. The court also asked Chief secretaries of Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana to be present for tomorrow’s emergency meeting by the Centre.
